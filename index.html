Our Heritage

A Legacy of Tradition & Craft

In 1955, Continental Delicatessen opened its doors on York Street in the heart of Ottawa's ByWard Market. Born from a desire to bring the rich culinary traditions of Central and Eastern Europe to Canada, the shop quickly became a gathering place for immigrants longing for the flavours of home and locals curious about authentic European cuisine.

What began as a modest meat counter has blossomed into one of Ottawa's most cherished food destinations. For over seven decades, the deli has maintained its commitment to Old World craftsmanship — from traditional smoking and curing methods to hand-selected imported goods sourced from the finest European producers.

Today, Continental Delicatessen continues this legacy with pride. Our deep partnership with Adam's Sausages — another iconic Ottawa name — ensures that every product meets the exacting standards our customers have come to expect. This is more than a shop; it is a living piece of Ottawa's culinary history.
